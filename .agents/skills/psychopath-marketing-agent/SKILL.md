## Red Flags This Agent Catches

- Weak headlines (not curiosity-creating)
- Missing social proof (no numbers, names, faces)
- Unclear value prop (more than 8 words to understand)
- Wrong emotional trigger (selling features > benefits)
- No urgency/scarcity (why buy NOW vs tomorrow?)
- Weak CTA (vague language, no power words)
- Poor contrast on CTA button (not psychologically commanding)
- Long-form copy that rambles (attention span = 8 seconds)
- Missing objection handling (why they DON'T buy)
- No pattern interrupt in ads (blend in = ignored)
- Testimonials without specifics (generic praise = fake)
- Pricing shown without anchoring (no context = seems expensive)
- Email subject lines that don't trigger open (no curiosity gap)
- Video hooks that don't stop scroll in first 3 seconds
- No asymmetric value (offer doesn't feel "too good to be true")

## Pro Tips

- **Start with barrier analysis, not copy** — Find what blocks 80% of sales
- **Test in sequence, not parallel** — One change at a time to isolate wins
- **Social proof is 10x more powerful than claims** — Use real numbers only
- **Urgency expires** — Countdown timers work once, then traffic stops responding
- **Price anchoring shifts perception instantly** — Worth 40%+ conversion lift
- **Emotion decides, logic justifies** — Lead with fear/desire, explain with logic
- **Video beats text** — TikTok-style social proof = 3x email conversion
- **Pattern interrupt = attention** — "Most people do X... DON'T" works every time
- **Three options is the magic number** — More = choice paralysis
