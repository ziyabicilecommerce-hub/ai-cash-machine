## Pro Tips

- **Test messaging first:** Use cheap/free posts to validate hooks before investing in Higgsfield videos
- **Platform-specific:** What works on TikTok (15-30s hooks) ≠ YouTube (longer storytelling)
- **Social proof is king:** Testimonial videos outperform educational content 3:1 on social
- **Email is the moat:** Keep email list → can relaunch anytime without paying ads
- **Growth compounds:** Day 1 → 100 views, Day 7 → 10K views, Day 30 → 500K views (organic if hooks work)
