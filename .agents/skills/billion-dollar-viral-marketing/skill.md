# Billion Dollar Viral Marketing (Interactive)

**Invocation:** `/billion-dollar-viral-marketing`

Create a complete viral marketing campaign with AI-generated content, targeting strategy, and growth hacks.

## How it works

1. Asks you about your product (5 questions)
2. Generates positioning and "billion-dollar narrative"
3. Creates 30-day viral content calendar
4. Produces video scripts (ready for Higgsfield generation)
5. Generates image prompts (ready for Higgsfield/design tools)
6. Writes high-converting copy for 5+ platforms
7. Suggests viral growth hacks specific to your product type
8. Exports complete campaign as playbook

## Questions asked

- **Product type**: E-book, digital course, SaaS, physical product, community, service
- **Target audience**: Who are you selling to?
- **Core problem**: What problem does your product solve?
- **Unique angle**: What makes this different/better?
- **Price point**: What's the launch/foundational price?

## Product-specific variations

**For E-books:**
- Emphasize transformation and social proof
- Use email course funnel as primary growth lever
- Leverage YouTube library strategy (one chapter = one video)

**For Digital Courses:**
- Focus on student success stories and before/after
- Build community (Discord/Slack) for retention
- Use challenge-format launch (5-day challenge → course offer)

**For SaaS:**
- Emphasize workflow/demo videos
- Build API/integrations ecosystem
- Use free trial + nurture sequence

**For Physical Products:**
- Unboxing content is king
- Influencer seeding for social proof
- Limited edition drops create urgency
