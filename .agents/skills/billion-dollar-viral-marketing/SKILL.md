# Billion Dollar Viral Marketing

Generate viral marketing campaigns for any product using AI-powered content creation. Works with e-books, digital products, physical products, and SaaS.

## When to use this skill

Use `/billion-dollar-viral-marketing` when you want to:
- Build a complete viral marketing strategy for a product
- Generate social media content (videos, images, carousel posts)
- Create landing page copy that converts
- Design email sequences that drive sales
- Produce TikTok/Reels/Shorts scripts
- Build a "billion-dollar positioning" narrative
- Create behind-the-scenes content
- Generate user testimonial prompts
- Build influencer outreach templates

## How it works

The skill uses a 7-step framework:

1. **Product Analysis** — Extracts the unique value proposition, target audience, and viral angles
2. **Positioning** — Creates a "billion-dollar" narrative (why this product changes the game)
3. **Content Strategy** — Maps out 30-day viral content calendar with 5+ content pillars
4. **Higgsfield Integration** — Generates video/image assets via Higgsfield (TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts format)
5. **Copy Generation** — Creates high-converting copy for each platform (LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok captions, email)
6. **Growth Hacks** — Suggests 5+ viral loops (referral, UGC, collaboration, challenge, viral mechanics)
7. **Execution Plan** — Gives you a day-by-day playbook to launch

## Input format

Provide:
- **Product name** and description (what it does)
- **Target audience** (who buys this, pain point, dream outcome)
- **Product type**: e-book, digital-course, SaaS, physical-product, community, service
- **Price point** (affects positioning)
- **Unique angle** (what makes this different from competitors)

## Output format

```
## 🎯 Positioning (The Big Idea)
[Billion-dollar narrative + why this spreads]

## 📱 30-Day Content Calendar
[Week-by-week content pillars + themes]

## 🎬 Video Scripts (for Higgsfield generation)
[3-5 viral video concepts with exact scripts]

## 📸 Image Prompts (for Higgsfield generation)
[5+ image prompts for social media]

## ✍️ Copy Variations
- LinkedIn hook + value
- Twitter 280-char banger
- TikTok hook + call to action
- Email subject line + opening

## 🚀 Growth Hacks
[5+ specific viral mechanics for this product type]

## 📅 Execution Checklist
[15-item launch playbook]
```

## Example: E-Book Viral Launch

**Input:**
- E-book: "The $10K/Month Copywriting Blueprint"
- Audience: Aspiring copywriters, freelancers
- Unique angle: "Reverse-engineering high-paying clients' ad copy"

**Output generates:**
- Social videos: "I made $150K analyzing 10,000 ads" (hook) + breakdown
- Email sequences: Cold outreach from copywriters who bought + testimonials
- Growth hack: Free email course teaser → upsell $47 e-book → $497 coaching
- Influencer templates: "How to pitch this to copywriting YouTube channels"

## Pro tips

- **For e-books**: Focus on transformation and social proof. Use before/after testimonials.
- **For SaaS**: Show workflow/demo videos. Emphasize problem-solution-result.
- **For physical products**: Unboxing videos, lifestyle shots, user-generated content loops.
- **For digital courses**: Student success stories. "Day in the life" of someone using the course.
- **For communities**: Member highlights. Problem you're solving at scale.
